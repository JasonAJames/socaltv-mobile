It's National {{ nationalday }} Day

{{nationalday}} Day is celebrated annually on the 16th August. {{nationalday}} Day is that the date was chosen to mark the 16th August, 1898, which was when the very first patent for a rollercoaster ride was issued. The date has been marked by fairgrounds and fans of rollercoasters all over the world since the eighties. Knott’s Berry Farm has the first and only dive coaster in California so it’s easy to see why we get excited about National Roller Coaster Day.
